Patricia's Favorite Movies

Top 3 movies listed:

The Mask of Zorro

This is my favorite movie as a kid. I love the Zorro lore and all the romance and action!

Under the Tuscun Sun

This movie is a classic. Makes you want to drop everything and go buy a fixer upper home in rural Italy. Diane Lane is great in this movie!

Thor: Love and Thunder

One of my favorite Marvel movies. The Thor series is hilarious, but the story plot of the movie is told beautifully. Theres lots of comedy and action in this film.
